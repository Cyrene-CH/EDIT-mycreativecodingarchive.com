Symbiosis

The big concept behind this project is the symbiosis and coexistence of the two ends of the world. Users will play the role of a fish, and their goal is to clean the trash cans and avoid the toxic bubbles while catching Oxgen bubbles to maintain living. An only tragegic ending was designed as the interactive game proceeds, where the tree and the fish are doomed to extinction. The hope is to warn people of the significance to protect natural ecosystems.
